================================================================
muffinplot EXAMPLES
================================================================

A selection of example uses of the different MATLAB plotting functions are provided, plus associated configuration setting files.

Refer to the comments in each function file for details.

The functions are:

fun_make_analysis_phys          --  basic ocean climatology (surface and benthic T and S, sea-ice) and circulation (vector currents, PSI, OPSI)
fun_make_analysis_phys_OPSI     --  means of plotting the meridional overturning stream-function for a region specified by a mask
fun_make_analysis_geochem       --  basic dissolved nutrient and oxygen distributions -- lon-lat surface and benthic slices, and latitude-vertical zonal means  
fun_make_analysis_geochem_ZONAL --  means of plotting specific basin of transect zonal means by specifying a mask
fun_make_analysis_bio           --  simple biological export diagnostics (POC and CaCO3 export patterns, CaCO3/POC rain ratio distribtuion)
fun_make_analysis_seds          --  basic seafloor fluxes and geochemsitry, sediment composition

fun_make_analysis_TS            --  an example of plotting time-series
